A.B.S

Alternative Biological Sciences

ABS is a workshop conducted by BioMe. It helps in students overall development by making them understand and learn theoretical science and giving them a platform to apply them practically. It helps students to think outside of the box. It teaches science in an alternative way through research approaches. It develops critical thinking, logical reasoning and the way one should approach science.

A.P.R.D

Awareness Programs on Rare Diseases

More than 300 million people worldwide are living with one of the 7000 rare diseases. A rare disease is any disease that affects a small percentage of the population. Most rare diseases are genetic and thus are present throughout the person's entire life, even if symptoms do not appear immediately. In India, more than 9 Crore people suffer from a rare disease and there is almost 40% chance of a wrong diagnosis.
