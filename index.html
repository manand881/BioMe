Get To Know Us Better

Welcome to BioMe Life Science

BioMe is a platform for young minds to explore science in a fun way. It helps students to think out of the box. It teaches science in an alternative way through research approaches. It develops critical thinking, logical reasoning and the way one should approach science.

Who We Are

We are biotechnologists who understand the importance of practical knowledge. We have a passion for biology that was gift given to us by various mentors over the years of our education. We wish to be those mentors for the next generation. We understand the inquisitiveness of young minds and want to help them satiate their thirst for knowledge. We as BioMe, envision young students to take more interest in the sciences and hope to see many more budding scientists.

Recognized by the Telangana State Innovation Cell

BioMe is recognized by the Telangana State Innovation Cell (TSIC) for its innovative research and development activities. We are a part of the TSIC as a part of the "Innovation and Technology" program.
