Who we are

We are biotechnologists who understand the importance of practical knowledge. We have a passion for biology that was gift given to us by various mentors over the years of our education. We wish to be those mentors for the next generation. We understand the inquisitiveness of young minds and want to help them satiate their thirst for knowledge. We as BioMe, envision young students to take more interest in the sciences and hope to see many more budding scientists.

What we’re looking for

We're looking for students from life sciences who can create exciting science modules and write technical content for us.

Qualification

Any domain from Life Sciences and Biotechnology.

Any one who is having a great passion for biology are welcome to apply for internship irrespective of their branch

Duration

We're looking for students who can commit to a minimum of 6 months internship from the date of joining.
